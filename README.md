This is Souptastic, a desk pet and fun video player! It sits on your desk and has Soup's likeness trapped inside!   
Loaded with fun scenes and animations, it is sure to make your desk feel more alive.   

# What it does   
Souptastic contains the face of Soup as a pet as an idle animation. Its two buttons that can be interacted with will allow you to select an animation/interactive video, eg. SOUP DATING!!! You can date three characters, each with unique interactions.

# Why it does   
Why not? It's soup!   
The inspiration behind this was the [Emo robot desk pet](https://living.ai/emo/). The idle animation was especially inspired by it, however the animations were completely original (+no AI involved in their creation). The stocky build was a nod to both Emo and Soup's fat body, and the ears are of course Soup's.

# How to assemble   
1. Build the box, or use your own!
2. Assemble the breadboard using the following wiring diagram: (insert)
3. Flash the ESP with the firmware.
4. Place the buttons through the box and keep the breadboard face stuck to a position where the screen is attached, preferably with electric tape.
5. Plug in a USB-C and connect your laptop to it. Run the software on the laptop. Make sure to prevent it from turning off. Download the gifs.
6. Run it and have fun!
